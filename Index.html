How Roblox Was Made/Introduction to Roblox

Roblox is an online cross-platform block game where you
can build and create anything you imagine to. It is known
for being one of the most played games in the world.
It has 230M+ accounts registered and has 500M+ downloads
on the google play app. It is estimated to have at least
20M online players daily (2023).
Roblox was founded in 2004. The founders of
roblox were named David Baszucki and Erik Cassel.
The main headquarters are currently located in San Mateo,
California. The first couple hundred builders of Roblox
were all developers, investors, testers, and friends of the founders.
