How Roblox Was Made/Introduction to Roblox

Roblox is an online cross-platform block game where you
can build and create anything you imagine to. It is known
for being one of the most played games in the world.
It has 230M+ accounts registered and has 500M+ downloads
on the google play app. It is estimated to have at least
20M online players daily (2023).
Roblox was founded in 2004. The founders of
roblox were named David Baszucki and Erik Cassel.
The main headquarters are currently located in San Mateo,
California. The first couple hundred builders of Roblox
were all developers, investors, testers, and friends of the founders.

What is in Roblox?

Roblox has tens of millions of games on the platform. These games are all made by Roblox developers.
Roblox developers are just normal people who go onto Roblox And download the Roblox studio app to
create any game they want. Remember, in roblox, anyone can create games which is the reason why there
are so many. You can also change your avatar and use roblox's ingame currency called robux to purchase new accessories,
faces and more! You can even buy some robux from the roblox store including
robux giftcards from the real life stores. Robux is not only used for changing
your ingame look, but you can buy gamepasses from games.(thats how people
make robux from making games but keep in mind, there is a 30% robux tax!). The best part about making a game is,

  • There is no limit to making games
  • You rarely have to write any code;just search up an object in Roblox's studio app and place it anywhere!
  • You can easily make robux by developing a game
